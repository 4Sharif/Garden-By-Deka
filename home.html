Welcome To Our Garden!

Welcome to Garden By Deka, a place where we record the day‑to‑day progress of Deka’s garden and share ideas for fellow plant lovers. Each section of the site highlights what’s growing, what’s blooming, and the simple techniques we use to keep the beds thriving through every season. Browse the pages and gather ideas for your own space. To learn more, view our About page.

About

Discover Our Page and Contact Us

Our site breaks down Deka's garden in its entirety. Check out the Tour page, where you'll see flower pots, how she maintains her garden beds, all the decorations set up, and more. In the Gallery section, you'll be able to view the best photos taken each month. If you want to follow Deka on her journey, go to her dedicated TikTok page where she posts consistently. If you have any more questions about this site, send us an email.
