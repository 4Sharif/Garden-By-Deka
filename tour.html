Explore Our Garden

There are many things to explore, and visitors can move through at their own pace. Our garden beds hold a variety of vegetables, like tomatoes and peppers. They're grown in compost and watered consistently. Along the walkway, a series of large containers display herbs and flowers chosen for pollinator support and ease of maintenance. Decorative features such as colorful signs, poles, and low‑voltage lighting characterize each area for a boost of personality. Some notes are presented for labeling names and dates, along with basic requirements for anyone who would like to replicate the results. The goal of the tour is to show how to keep the space productive within seasons while maintaining a clean, approachable layout.
